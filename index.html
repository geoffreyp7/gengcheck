Important Notes


Does not work in Safari, only tested with Google Chrome and Firefox. Your program must output in the format listed in the specs or this will not work. After submitting your program output to be checked the results of the check will be downloaded, the page will then display an error, ignore this! No polynomials generated in the test data will have roots closer than 0.1. It is not recommended to open the files with notepad as it will display everything on one line.
